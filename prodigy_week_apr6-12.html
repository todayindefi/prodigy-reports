Areas for Improvement

  • URL clicks dropped 10% (10 → 9) despite 42% more impressions. CTR is falling — tweets are engaging but not converting to site visits. The top 2 tweets by impressions had zero clicks.
  • Still no new follows from any tweet. Fourth straight week without a follow-driving tweet. The content is resonating with existing audience but not attracting new eyeballs.
  • Tweet ceiling still under 250 impressions. Improvement over last week’s 144 cap, but still well below the 400+ ceilings from mid-March. The account needs a breakout moment.
  • The “I DARE YOU” all-caps tweet underperformed — 84 impressions, near bottom of the week. Aggressive/clickbait tone doesn’t suit this audience. Compare to the measured “structured notes” tweet at 213.
